Tacos

Celebrate 150 years with this quintessential Canadian Taco

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp (15 mL) vegetable oil
  • 1 lb (500 g) ground beef
  • 1 pkg Old El Pasoâ„¢ Stand and Stuff Tacos Dinner Kit
  • 2 tbsp (30 mL) Canadian maple syrup
  • 2 cups (500 mL) shredded lettuce

Steps

  1. In large skillet, heat oil over medium heat; cook beef and taco seasoning mix, stirring occasionally, for 8 to 10 minutes or until browned and cooked through. Stir in maple syrup.
  2. Spoon beef mixture into taco shells. Top with lettuce, cheese, tomatoes, taco sauce, bacon, cilantro and green onion.
